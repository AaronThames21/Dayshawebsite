ALUMNI WEEKEND 2025

WCT / C.E. Murray

5th Annual Alumni Weekend

October 10–12, 2025

Theme: Hawaiian Luau

We are excited this year to celebrate WCT/C.E. Murray 5th Annual Alumni Weekend, October 10–12, 2025. We will kick off the celebration with a parade. The theme for the entire weekend is a Hawaiian Luau.

Though our vision is to provide a Hawaiian Luau theme experience, there are guidelines that participants are expected to follow. These guidelines have been developed to provide a structured and safe parade experience for all in attendance. Individuals are expected to conduct themselves in a manner consistent with the nature and character of C.E. Murray High School. We want the parade experience to be enjoyed by all.

Parade Details

  • Line Up
    3:45 PM
    New gym (James Dingle Gymnasium) parking lot of C.E. Murray Elementary/Middle School.
  • Start Time
    5:00 PM (prompt)
  • Route
    Begin at C.E. Murray Elementary/Middle School → left onto C.E. Murray Boulevard → right on Main Street → right on County Road S-45-57 → end at Greeleyville Primary School.

“Pink Out” — Friday, Oct 10

In recognition of Breast Cancer Awareness Month, we celebrate alumni who are survivors, honor those still bravely fighting, and remember those we’ve lost. Show your support by wearing pink, adding pink décor, or even pink leis. Participation is optional, but encouraged.

New Policy in 2025

Town of Greeleyville Policy Update for WCT/CEM Alumni Weekend 2025

The Town of Greeleyville and the WCT/CEM Alumni Weekend Committee value the traditions and sense of community that the WCT/CEM Alumni Weekend brings each year. To ensure the safety and enjoyment of all participants, as well as to address liability concerns and previous complaints, the following policy for minors will be in effect for 2025.

Effective for WCT/CEM Alumni Weekend 2025

  • Friday, October 10, 2025: Minors under 18 may attend only if accompanied by a parent or legal guardian (18+) at all times, and must remain within the parent/guardian’s line of sight. Minors should not be dropped off at the event.
    • Minors are not allowed to loiter or congregate in the following areas: the steps behind the pavilion; the sides of the pavilion; restroom facilities near the town hall (except when in use); the front and sides of the town hall; the area between the town hall and the pavilion; the rear restrooms (except when in use); sidewalks; and parking lots.
    • If a minor uses the restroom, they must promptly return to their parent or legal guardian immediately afterward.
  • Saturday, October 11, 2025: Minors under 18 are not permitted to attend the festivities.

This policy is enacted to ensure safety, reduce disruptions, and maintain a family-friendly environment. Failure to adhere may result in legal action, including potential fines and/or arrest for non-compliance. For questions, please contact the Town of Greeleyville or a WCT/CEM Alumni Weekend Committee member.
